ABOUT US

Who Are We?

AID4ALL is an initiative run by students in Cambridge with help from partner organizations that seeks to improve the lives of unhoused people in the Waterloo Region. We seek your help to help us raise money and spread awareness about the inaccess many homeless people have to a decent standard of treatment for minor injuries.

Our Story

In 2025, we noticed a gap in support for those experiencing homelessness. While many programs provide food and shelter, access to essential health supplies was often overlooked. Determined to make a difference, we founded AID4ALL to ensure that everyone in our community could receive the care they need to stay healthy and safe, especially those who are disadvantaged.
