OUR MISSION

Our mission

AID4ALL is a student-led not-for-profit organisation whose goal is to distribute first aid kits to people experiencing homelessness. A simple cut or infection should never escelate to a crisis due to inaccess to clean and proper medical supplies, but this is the unfortunate reality for many. By giving out first aid kits through our partners, AID4ALL seeks to improve the day-to-day lives of these people and send them one simple message: their health and safety matters.

How Can You Help?

To help us better those who live in our community, you can make a donation or spread awareness about our campaign. Even the donating price of a coffee or letting others know about our mission would help us reach our goal. Our cause relies on the support of our community, so simply expressing your support for our goal contributes!
