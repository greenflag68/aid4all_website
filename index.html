What is AID4ALL

AID4ALL is a student-led not-for-profit committed to supporting people experiencing homelessness. Through partnerships and community outreach, we provide essential resources like first aid kits to help improve daily life and promote health and safety. Our work is guided by the belief that everyone deserves access to basic care and the dignity that comes with it.

Where Can You Donate

To donate to AID4ALL, you can contact us or you can reach out to us in person.

What goes into each first aid kit?

In every first aid kit we include:

  • Gauze rolls
  • Alcohol Prep Pads
  • Bandaids
  • Nitrile Gloves
  • Masks
  • Micropore Tape

These important items help people afflicted with homelessness address medical issues, ensuring their safety.
